Initial Design

Prototype 1 was created to test the basic dimensions and fit of the ESP32-C3 Zero board.

Features

  • Simple enclosure design
  • Open top section to allow access to the buttons

Problems Identified

  • Does not protect the board around the buttons
  • The basic dimensions may need adjustment

3D Print Results

Testing showed that due to "overhang" issues, the case can not be printed as intended.
