Project Overview

This website documents the design and development of a protective case for the ESP32-C3 Zero microcontroller. The project progressed through two prototypes before reaching the final design.

Design Goals

  • Protect the ESP32-C3 Zero board
  • Allow easy access to USB and GPIO pins
  • Provide a secure fit
  • Support cooling and airflow
